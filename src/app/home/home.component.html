About Us

Today, Wells Fargo is a leading financial services company with approximately $1.9 trillion in assets. In the U.S., it serves one in three households and more than 10% of small businesses, and is a leading middle-market banking provider. Wells Fargo works to create positive social impact in the communities it serves by supporting housing affordability, small business growth, financial health, and a low-carbon economy.
